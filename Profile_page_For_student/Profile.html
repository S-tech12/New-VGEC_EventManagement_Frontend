Instructions

Welcome! Please read the instructions carefully before proceeding.

For Event Hosts:
  • Submit your event proposal at least 15 days before the event date.
  • Verify all event details (title, date, time, venue, description) before submitting. Once submitted, you cannot update it.
  • Events can only be Added or Deleted; you cannot Approve or Update events.
  • Track your event status: Pending, Approved, or Rejected through your dashboard.
  • If Rejected, you may re-submit with corrections if time allows.
  • If the event is Approved but Not Completed, you cannot delete the event or your account.
  • If your event is Pending and the HOD deletes their account, your event will also be deleted. You’ll be notified via email.
  • Enrollment is not allowed on the event day. Students must enroll beforehand.
  • If the event is marked Paid, the host must collect fees manually. (No online method for now.)
  • You can Download Participant Files from your Profile Page.
  • Certificates can be generated by students after event completion. A sample certificate is provided for reference.
  • Refer to FAQs in your Profile Page for more clarification.
For Students:
  • Each event card shows a badge to indicate whether it's Free or Paid.
  • To enroll in a Paid Event, you must complete payment via online Razorpay integration
  • After a successful payment, a Fee Receipt PDF will be sent to your registered email and you can also download from your profile section.
  • If you enroll but do not attend any event (Free or Paid), your account will be blocked from future enrollments.
  • Certificates can only be generated after the event is marked as Completed by the host.
  • Carefully read all event details before enrolling — you must meet any eligibility criteria.
  • Enrollment closes before the event day; no enrollments allowed on the event day itself.
